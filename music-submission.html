DEMO SUBMISSIONS

We are constantly searching for alternative, unique, and uncompromising voices. Review our submission criteria below and share your sound.

Demo Guidelines

  • 01.

    Streaming Links Only: We do not accept attached mp3 files. Please upload your track to Soundcloud (private links preferred) or Dropbox.

  • 02.

    Unreleased Material: We prioritize tracks that are fully unreleased or upcoming releases seeking marketing support.

  • 03.

    Context Matters: Write a brief introductory bio. Tell us where you are based and what your creative vision is.

FAQ

Our Workflow

A&R Evaluation Pipeline

01. INTAKE

Demo Logged

Your streaming URL, lyrics context, and social presence details are logged in our weekly listen queue.

02. LISTEN

A&R Evaluation

Our creative directors and A&R agents screen submissions during our bi-weekly group playback sessions.

03. OUTREACH

Direct Contact

If a track resonates, we reach out directly to request uncompressed audio files and arrange a video meet.

04. PARTNER

Contract Offer

We outline potential deals covering physical releases, sync licensing campaigns, and tour booking agency support.

Tactile Philosophy

Tactile Formats & Manufacturing Ethos

180g Virgin Wax

We manufacture our records exclusively on audiophile-grade 180g heavy virgin vinyl to reduce sound distortion and prevent warping over time.

Chrome Audio Tapes

Our cassette runs use high-bias chrome formulations (Type II) housed in custom tinted library cases, providing warm magnetic frequency response.

FSC Certified Sleeves

Environmental sustainability matters. All jackets, liners, and lyric sheets use FSC-certified recycled paper printed with organic soy-based inks.

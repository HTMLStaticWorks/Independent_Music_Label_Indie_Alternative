Silent Satellites

AMNESIA SKY

Released October 2025 • Echo Chamber Records (EC-2025-04)

The highly anticipated full-length debut from Silent Satellites is now available in high-fidelity physical formats. Recorded over a twelve-week period in our London analog studios, the album features sweeping guitars, custom synth textures, and visceral drum lines.
